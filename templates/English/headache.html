 Headache

  Ingridents:

  • Ksheerabala Taila
  • Balashwagandhadi Taila
  • Mahanarayana Taila
  • Migrakot oil
  • Nasya(nasal drop)

  Procedure:

  1. Take a few drops of the oil on to finger tips. Gently rub the oil using finger tips in circular manner over forehead and temple region.
  2. Continue massage for 2-10 minutes,based on your comport. Close your eyes and relax
  3. Nasya is the procedure of instilling medicated nasal drops through nose. As in siddha is considered as pathway of head,this procedure is highly effective in curing diseases particularly from above shoulder region.
  4. Medicines for Nasya in tension headache- Anu taila,Ksheerabala101,Ksheerabala taila,Danvantari taila.

 Sinusitis Headache

  Ingridents:

  • Vasanthakusumakaram mathirai
  • Kasturi karuppu
  • Gowrichinthamani chendooram
  • Chandamarutha chendooram
  • Sivanaramirtham
  • Seenthil chooranam
  • Mandoora chendooram

  Procedure:

  1. The oral medicines should be taken with appropriate anupanam depending upon the severity of the disease and body constitution of the patient .
  2. Steam inhalation with lemon leaves or thumbai samoolam boiled in water is used. Steam is effective in opening nasal passages, thereby aiding in easy drainage of mucous from the sinuses.
