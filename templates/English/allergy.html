Allergies

 Insect Allergy.

  Ingridents:

  • Cinnamon
  • Papaya
  • Onion
  • Peppermint
  • Aloe Vera

  Procedure:

  1. Cinnamon spice has anti-bacterial and anti-inflammatory properties that provide instant relief. Crush some cinnamon and mix it with water to make a paste. Apply the mix on the infected area leaving it for about an hour.
  2. The enzymes in this Papaya help in neutralising the insect venom. Lay a slice of this fruit on the sting for about an hour for relief.
  3. Onion vegetable contains enzymes that aid in breaking down the inflammatory compounds from the bite. Cut an onion and rub it directly on the sting till the itchiness reduces.
  4. The cooling sensation offered by fresh peppermint leaves or even essential oil help provide temporary relief by reducing the itching. Simply place the crushed leaves or apply the oil over the bite for about 15 minutes.
  5. The antiseptic properties in aloe vera make it a great remedy for insect bites. Apply some juice or gel directly over the bite for relief.
